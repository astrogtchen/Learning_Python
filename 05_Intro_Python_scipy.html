Planck Function¶

在任意溫度T下，從一個黑體中發射的電磁輻射的輻射率與電磁輻射的頻率的關係式。 在單位時間內從單位表面積和單位立體角內以單位頻率間隔或單位波長間隔輻射出的能量

$$ B_{\nu}(\nu, T) = \frac{2h \nu^3}{c^2} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{h\nu}{kT}}-1} $$

$$ B_{\lambda}(\lambda, T) = \frac{2hc^2}{\lambda^5} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{hc}{\lambda kT}}-1}$$

式中 h = Planck constant，k = Boltzmann constant，c = speed of light in vaccum

The SI units of $B_{\nu}$ are $ W·sr^{−1}·m^{−2}·Hz^{−1}$, while those of $B_{\lambda}$ are $W·sr^{−1}·m^{−3}$

所以單位體積、單位頻率的能量 spectral energy density (u) $$ u_{\nu}(\nu, T) = \frac{4\pi}{c} B_{\nu}(\nu, T) = \frac{8\pi h \nu^3}{c^3} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{h\nu}{kT}}-1} $$

$$ u_{\lambda}(\lambda, T) = \frac{4\pi}{c} B_{\lambda}(\lambda, T) = \frac{8\pi hc}{\lambda^5} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{hc}{\lambda kT}}-1}$$

Energy density¶

$$\frac{U}{V} = \int_{0}^{\infty} u_{\nu}(\nu, T) d\nu = \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{8\pi h \nu^3}{c^3} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{h\nu}{kT}}-1} d\nu = \frac{4\sigma T^4}{c}$$

令 $ x = \frac{h\nu}{kT} $，故 $dx = \frac{h}{kT} d\nu$ $$\frac{U}{V} = \frac{8\pi (kT)^4}{(hc)^3} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^3}{e^{x}-1} dx $$

此積分項稱Bose-Einstein integral, 可利用 Riemann zeta function $ \zeta (4)$ 與 Gamma function 求值： $$ J = \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^3}{e^{x}-1} dx = \zeta(4) \Gamma(4) =\frac{\pi^4}{90} \times 6 =\frac{\pi^4}{15} $$

Riemann zeta function$: \zeta(s) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^{s}}$

Gamma function: $ \Gamma(n) = \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^{n-1}}{e^{x}} dx = (n-1) !$

故

$$\frac{U}{V} = \frac{8 \pi^5 (kT)^4}{15(hc)^3} =aT^4 $$ 其中 $a$ 稱為radiation constant

$$ a = 7.56 \times 10^{-16} Jm^{-3}K^{-4}$$

計算過程¶

ref :林文隆，物理雙月刊（23卷2期）2001年4月，宇宙能量密度與黎曼$\zeta$函數

$$ \frac{1}{e^{x}-1} = \frac{1}{e^{x} \left( 1-e^{-x} \right) } = e^{-x} \left( 1+e^{-x}+e^{-2x}+ \cdots \right) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} e^{-nx}$$

$$ J = \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^3}{e^{x}-1} dx = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} x^{3} dx$$

adopt integration by part

$$ \int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} x^{3} dx = \frac{3}{n}\int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} x^{2} dx = \frac{3 \cdot 2}{n^2}\int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} x dx =\frac{3 \cdot 2 \cdot 1}{n^3}\int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} dx = \frac{3!}{n^{3}} \times \frac{1}{n} = \frac{3!}{n^4}$$

$$ \therefore J=3! \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^4} = 3! \zeta \left( 4 \right)$$

黎曼$\zeta$函數： $$\zeta(s) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^{s}} $$

當$s>1$時函數收斂。當$s$為偶數時，可利用傅立葉級數求值：

$$ x^{2} = \frac{\pi^2}{3} + 4 \sum_{1}^{\infty} (-1)^{n} \frac{\cos nx}{n^2}$$

$$ x^{4} = \frac{\pi^4}{5} + \sum_{1}^{\infty} (-1)^{n} \left( \frac{8\pi^2}{n^2} - \frac{48}{n^4} \right) \cos nx$$

令 $x = \pi $ 代入上兩式， $$ \pi^2 = \frac{\pi^2}{3} + 4 \sum_{1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} \Rightarrow \zeta(2)= \sum_{1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2}= \frac{\pi^2}{6}$$

$$ \frac{4}{5} \pi^4 = 8 \pi^2 \zeta(2) - 48 \zeta(4) \Rightarrow \zeta(4) = \frac{\pi^4}{90}$$

所以，

$$J = 3! \zeta \left( 4 \right) = \frac{\pi^4}{15}$$

或是利用Parseval's identity:

f(x)之傅立葉級數為 $$ f(x) = \frac{a_{0}}{2} + \sum_{1}^{\infty}a_{n}\cos nx + \sum_{1}^{\infty}b_{n} \sin nx ，(-\pi<x<\pi)$$

Parseval's identity $$ \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} \lbrack f(x) \rbrack^{2} dx = \frac{{a_{0}}^{2}}{2} + \sum_{1}^{\infty} \left( {a_{n}}^2 + {b_{n}}^2 \right)$$

令$f(x)=x^2$代入，且已知各係數值 $a_{0}= \frac{2}{3}\pi^2$，$a_{n}=\frac{4(-1)^n}{n^2}$

$$ \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} \lbrack x^2 \rbrack^{2} dx = \frac{(\frac{2}{3}\pi^2)^2}{2} + \sum_{1}^{\infty} (-1)^{2n}\frac{16}{n^4}$$

$$\frac{1}{\pi} \frac{x^5}{5} \bracevert_{-\pi}^{\pi}= \frac{2}{5}\pi^4 = \frac{2}{9}\pi^4 + \sum_{1}^{\infty} \frac{16}{n^4} \Rightarrow \zeta(4) = \frac{\pi^4}{90} $$

Stefan-Boltzmann law:¶

The Stefan–Boltzmann law gives the power emitted per unit area of the emitting body,

$$\frac{P}{A} = \int_{0}^{\infty} B_{\nu}(\nu, T)d\nu \int d\Omega$$

Lambert's cosine law: the intensity observed along the sphere will be the actual intensity times the cosine of the zenith angle φ,

$$\frac{P}{A} =\int_{0}^{\infty} B_{\nu}(\nu, T)d\nu \int_{0}^{2\pi} d\theta \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \cos \phi \sin \phi d\phi = \pi \int_{0}^{\infty} B_{\nu}(\nu, T)d\nu = \frac{c}{4} \frac{U}{V} = \sigma T^{4} $$

$$\sigma = \frac{2\pi^5 k^4}{15c^2 h ^3} = \frac{ac}{4} = 5.67\times 10^{-8} Js^{-1}m^{-2}K^{-4}$$

單位體積的光子數量¶

$$ n = \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{u_{\nu}(\nu, T)}{h\nu} d\nu = \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{8\pi \nu^2}{c^3} \frac{1}{e^{\frac{h\nu}{kT}}-1} d\nu $$

令$ x = \frac{h\nu}{kT} $，故 $dx = \frac{h}{kT} d\nu$ $$\frac{N}{V} = \frac{8\pi (kT)^3}{(hc)^3} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^2}{e^{x}-1} dx $$

積分項：

$$ N = \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^2}{e^{x}-1} dx = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} x^{2} dx $$

$$ \int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} x^{2} dx = \frac{2}{n}\int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} x dx = \frac{2 \cdot 1}{n^2}\int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-nx} dx = \frac{2!}{n^{2}} \times \frac{1}{n} = \frac{2!}{n^3}$$

故$$N = 2 \times \zeta(3)$$

而 $\zeta(3)\approx 1.202 $，所以$N \approx 2.404$

$$ n = \frac{8\pi (kT)^3}{(hc)^3} \times 2.404 \approx 20.2 T^3 photons/cm^3 $$

宇宙微波背景輻射的溫度$T=2.7$K， 故$$n(T=2.7K) \approx 400 photons/cm^3$$

相對論性費米子的能量密度與數量密度¶

光子的能量分佈函數遵守Bose-Einstein distribution $$f_{B}= \frac{1}{e^{E/kT}-1}$$ 而對於半整數自旋的費米子，其能量分佈函數為Fermi-Dirac distribution，

$$f_{f}= \frac{1}{e^{E/kT}+1}$$

故計算費米子的能量密度時，會有 $$ u_{f} = a T^4 \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^3}{e^{x}+1}dx$$ 其中$$J_{F}=\int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^3}{e^{x}+1}dx $$ 又設$J_{B}=\int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^3}{e^{x}-1}dx $ $$ \Rightarrow J_{B}-J_{F}= \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{2x^3}{e^{2x}-1} dx $$

令$y=2x$，則

$$ J_{B}-J_{F}= \frac{1}{8} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{y^3}{e^{y}-1} dy = \frac{1}{8}J_{B}$$

$$ \Rightarrow J_{F}=\frac{7}{8}J_{B} $$

推廣：

$$ \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{e^{x}+1} dx = \left( 1- \frac{1}{2^n} \right) \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{e^{x}-1} dx $$

故$$ N_{F}=\int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^2}{e^{x}+1}dx ，N_{B}=\int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x^2}{e^{x}-1}dx \Rightarrow N_{F}=\frac{3}{4}N_{B}$$

所以，

  • 相對論性波色子、光子能量密度 $u_{B}=aT^{4}$

    相對論性費米子能量密度 $u_{F}= \frac{7}{8}u_{B}= \frac{7}{8}aT^4$

  • 相對論性波色子、光子數量密度 $n_{B}=bT^{3}$

    相對論性費米子能量密度 $n_{F}= \frac{3}{4}n_{B}= \frac{3}{4}bT^3$

SHM¶

$$ m \ddot{x} + b \dot{x} + kx = A \sin(x) $$

$$ \Rightarrow \ddot{x} + \frac{b}{m} \dot{x} + \frac{k}{m}x = \frac{A}{m} \sin(x)$$

define $\omega_0 = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}，\omega_{b}=\sqrt{\frac{b}{2m}}，a = \frac{A}{m}$

$$ \ddot{x} + 2\omega_{b}^{2}\dot{x} +\omega_{0}^{2}x = a \sin(x)$$

let $ u=\dot{x}$, then

import numpy as np
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

m = 5 
k = 1
b = 0.01 
A = 0.0 

OM0 = np.sqrt(k/m)
OMb = np.sqrt(b/2*m)
a = A/m

IC = [0, 5] #initial condition=[x0(m),v0(m/s)]

def dU_dx(U,t):
    # x=U[0] and x'=u=U[1]. This function should return [x', u'=x'']
    return [U[1], (-(OMb**2)*U[1]-(OM0**2)*U[0]) + a*np.sin(U[0])]

Solving ODE by finite difference method¶

SHM:¶

$$\vec{a} = -\omega^{2} \vec{x}$$

設時間間隔為$\Delta t$ 的SHM物體連續三點位置座標：$x_{i-1}，x_{i}，x_{i+1}$ $$a = \lim_{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac{v_{k+1}-v_{k}}{\Delta t}$$

其中 $$v_{k}=\lim_{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac{x_{i}-x_{i-1}}{\Delta t}，v_{k+1}=\lim_{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac{x_{i+1}-x_{i}}{\Delta t}$$

所以 $$a = \lim_{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac{ \frac{x_{i+1}-x_{i}}{\Delta t}- \frac{x_{i}-x_{i-1}}{\Delta t}}{\Delta t}$$

$$\Rightarrow a = (x_{i+1}-2x_{i}+x_{i-1}) \Delta t^{-2} =\omega^{2}x_{i}$$

$$\Rightarrow (x_{i+1}-2x_{i}+x_{i-1}) =- \omega^{2}\Delta t ^{2}x_{i}= - (\frac{2\pi \Delta t }{T})^{2}x_{i} $$

設 $T =2 \pi$， $\Delta t = 0.1 $

$$ (x_{i+1}-2x_{i}+x_{i-1}) =- 0.01 x_{i} \Rightarrow x_{i+1}=1.99x_{i}-x_{i-1}$$

得到位置的遞迴關係式，$ i \rightarrow i+1$ 時間間隔為$\Delta t =0.1$

設 $x_{0}=0，x_{1} = 0.1$，可由遞迴關係式得到各時間點的位置座標

擬合 fitting¶

from scipy.optimize import curve_fit

Use non-linear least squares to fit a function, f, to data.

Assumes ydata = f(xdata, *params) + eps

scipy.optimize.curve_fit(fn, xdata, ydata, p0=())

fn : model function p0 : Initial guess for the parameters. If None, then the initial values will all be 1

return:

  • popt : array

Optimal values for the parameters so that the sum of the squared residuals of f(xdata, *popt) - ydata is minimized

  • pcov : 2d array

The estimated covariance of popt. The diagonals provide the variance of the parameter estimate. To compute one standard deviation errors on the parameters use perr = np.sqrt(np.diag(pcov)).

https://docs.scipy.org/doc/scipy/reference/generated/scipy.optimize.curve_fit.html

%matplotlib inline

from scipy.optimize import curve_fit
import numpy as np
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

# create data with noise
time = np.linspace(0, 10, 200)
data = 50 * np.sin(2 * np.pi / 2.5 * time) + 100  +  np.random.normal(0, 5., len(time))
plt.plot(time, data, 'k.')

data_err = 4 * np.random.rand(len(time)) + 1
plt.errorbar(time, data, yerr=data_err, fmt="r")


def model(time, amplitude, period, dc):
    return amplitude * np.sin(2 * np.pi / period * time) + dc

popt, pcov = curve_fit(model, time, data,  p0=[50, 2.5, 100], sigma=data_err)

print(popt)
print(pcov)

perr = np.sqrt(np.diag(pcov))

print("Amplitude =", popt[0], "+/-", perr[0])
print("Period =", popt[1], "+/-", perr[1])
print("DC =", popt[2], "+/-", perr[2])

plt.errorbar(time, data, yerr=data_err, fmt="none", label='Data')
plt.plot(time, model(time, popt[0], popt[1], popt[2]),'r-', label='Model')
plt.xlabel('Time')
plt.ylabel('data')
plt.legend(loc='best')
